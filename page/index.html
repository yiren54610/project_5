How Tall Are The Characters in Animations?

Anime characters can be very surreal. This story looks at the height of animation characters in real life and whether it correlates with their popularity.

By Yiren Lu

Gender representation in animation, movies, and TV shows has long been a central concern among critics of popular culture. This story takes a data-driven approach to the conversation by analyzing the most beloved characters on MyAnimeList. For all characters who got more than one thousand votes, male characters are more than twice of number of female characters.

For male characters, the average height is 171 cm, while for females, it's 161 cm.

Interestingly, male characters tend to have a greater variance of height.
By contrast, female characters are closer to real human heights.

For example, Charlotte Katakuri is the highest character in the series, with a height more than 5 meters, but his votes are only 6000 compared to Lelouche. .

Interestingly, male characters displayed a greater range of height distributions while the range for female characters tended to be narrower.
